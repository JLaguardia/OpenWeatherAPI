Frequently Asked Questions

  • How Do I Bookmark/Add A Location?

    To bookmark a location, touch/click an area on the map to add a pin and to the list at the bottom.

  • How Do I Remove Location Bookmark?

    To delete a bookmark, swipe left on the list. You can also touch/click the settings button >> "Delete all bookmarks"

  • How Do I Edit Location Bookmark?

    To rename a bookmark, you may either long-press/long-click on the location name in the list, or tap/click the marker to open the InfoWindow, and click/press the window itself to bring up the rename dialog.

  • How Do I View Location Details?

    To view the details of a location, simply touch/click a location on the list, or tap/click the marker to open the InfoWindow, and long-click/long-press the window itself.

  • How Do I Change The Unit Of Measurement?

    You may change the unit type by touching/clicking the settings button >> "Unit System" slider. The default is Imperial.
